Hotel facilities

Incorporating the pleasures of an unforgettable holiday experience, The Pegasus Reef Hotel in Wattala has evolved to offer a collection of 140 well-appointed rooms including a spacious suite designed to meet your every need. A perfect combination of contemporary architecture & sheer comfort, our rooms come with tailor-made amenities like a mini-bar, safety lockers, separate balconies, 24 hr on-call room service & much more.
Sink into the serenity of our blissful haven and enjoy every moment in a world of your own at one of the finest Colombo beach hotels.
